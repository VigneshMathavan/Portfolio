OVERVIEW

Tamil has 80M+ speakers. Zero audiobook platforms.

Tamil is spoken by over 80 million people worldwide — yet Tamil audiobook listeners have no dedicated platform. Content is scattered across YouTube, WhatsApp forwards, and obscure apps with no curation, no quality signal, and no discovery layer.

KadhaiSolai ("Story Garden" in Tamil) flips the production problem entirely: authors upload their Tamil text, and the platform converts it into a narrated audiobook using Sarvam AI's Bulbul v3 engine — the most natural-sounding Tamil TTS available — in under 20 minutes. No studio. No microphone. No technical knowledge required.

THE INSIGHT

"The bottleneck wasn't readership — it was production. Every Tamil author I spoke to wanted to reach audio audiences but couldn't afford a studio. AI narration removes that barrier entirely."

PROCESS

From problem to live product

01

STEP 01

User Research

Conducted 12+ interviews with Tamil audiobook listeners (ages 22–55) across India, UK, and UAE. Three themes dominated: broken discovery, no quality signal, and fragmented community. The supply side — Tamil authors — showed a clear willingness to produce if the barrier was low enough.

02

STEP 02

Product Strategy

Mapped the competitive landscape — Audible, Storytel, Kuku FM — and identified the Tamil-language gap. Chose AI narration over a marketplace model because it eliminated the supply-side bottleneck. Defined MVP scope: text upload → AI narration → listener streaming, with author profiles and genre discovery.

03

STEP 03

PRD & Build

Wrote the full PRD covering personas, user flows, success metrics, and technical requirements around Sarvam AI's Bulbul v3 TTS engine — which supports 8 distinct Tamil voice options (Anand, Kavitha, Vijay, Shruti, Priya, Pavithra, Neel, Arvind). Built and deployed the live product on Vercel.

04

STEP 04

Iteration

Ran usability sessions measuring task completion for author upload, voice selection, and listener streaming flows. Added speed control, progress tracking, and AI-assisted story creation tools based on feedback. Writing streaks and community genre feeds were added in the second sprint cycle.

SOLUTION

KadhaiSolai — what's live

AI Narration Engine — Authors upload Tamil text files. Sarvam Bulbul v3 converts them to natural-sounding audio in under 20 minutes: short stories (~1 min), novel chapters (~3 min), full novels (~15 min).

8 Voice Options — Listeners and authors choose from Anand, Kavitha, Vijay, Shruti, Priya, Pavithra, Neel, and Arvind — covering a range of tones, genders, and reading styles.

Discovery Layer — Curated genre feeds (Drama, Thriller, Poetry), author profiles with follower tracking, and a community recommendation surface so listeners can find content without hunting for it.
