OVERVIEW

The loneliness of watching alone

Netflix has 238M+ subscribers — but watching is still a fundamentally solo experience. Netflix Party (Teleparty) showed demand for shared viewing, yet it required a Chrome extension and felt like a workaround, not a feature.

Netflix Connect is a product case study exploring what a native co-watching and social entertainment layer inside Netflix could look like — designed to drive session frequency, retention, and the social proof that fuels content discovery.

THE OPPORTUNITY

"42% of Netflix subscribers regularly discuss shows with friends after watching. The conversation happens — just nowhere near Netflix's product. What if it happened inside it?"

PROCESS

From research to PRD

01

STEP 01

Research

Conducted secondary research across Netflix earnings calls, Teleparty data, and published UX studies. Ran 8 user interviews with 18–34 Netflix users. Identified 3 consistent behavioural patterns.

02

STEP 02

Persona Definition

Defined 3 primary personas: the Long-Distance Pair (couples/family separated by geography), the Friend Group Binge-Watcher, and the Solo Recommender who shares but doesn't co-watch.

03

STEP 03

Feature Scoping

Prioritised the MVP feature set using RICE scoring across 12 candidate features. Top features: Watch Party scheduling, spoiler-safe episode reactions, and a Friends Activity feed on the homepage.

04

STEP 04

PRD Writing

Produced a full PRD covering problem statement, success metrics (DAU on social features, referral-driven sign-ups), technical requirements, edge cases, and open questions for engineering.

SOLUTION

Netflix Connect — feature set

Watch Party Scheduler — Create a session, invite friends by Netflix ID, choose a date/time. Netflix handles synchronised playback and sends calendar invites.

Episode Reactions — Emoji + short text reactions attached to specific episode timestamps. Spoiler-gated until the viewer reaches that moment.

Friends Activity Feed — A dedicated tab showing what friends are watching, finished, and rating. Passive discovery surface driven by social proof.

SUCCESS METRICS

Primary: Weekly active users on social features (target 8% MAU within 6 months). Secondary: Session frequency for Watch Party users (+15% target). Guardrail: No increase in privacy-related support tickets.
