OVERVIEW

Tamil audiobooks have no home

Tamil is spoken by over 80 million people worldwide, yet Tamil audiobook listeners have no dedicated discovery platform. Content is scattered across YouTube, WhatsApp groups, and obscure apps — with no curation, ratings, or personalised recommendations.

KadhaiSolai ("Story Garden" in Tamil) is a focused discovery platform that helps Tamil readers find, track, and share audiobooks that matter to them.

WHY THIS PROBLEM

"I kept recommending audiobooks to family members and each time we'd spend 20 minutes trying to find a working link. Every Tamil listener I talked to had the same story."

PROCESS

Building 0 to 1

01

STEP 01

User Research

Ran 12+ interviews with Tamil audiobook listeners aged 22–55 across India, UK, and UAE. Synthesised 3 core themes: broken discovery, unclear quality, and fragmented community.

02

STEP 02

Product Strategy

Mapped the competitive landscape — Audible, Storytel, Kuku FM — and identified the Tamil-language gap. Defined MVP scope: catalog discovery + ratings + listening progress tracking.

03

STEP 03

PRD & Build

Wrote the full PRD covering personas, user flows, and success metrics. Built a functional prototype using Lovable + Supabase in 3 weeks. Iterated based on 5 beta testers over 3 sprint cycles.

04

STEP 04

Validation

Ran usability sessions measuring task completion for core flows: search, save to library, rate a book. 4.2★ avg satisfaction. Identified top 3 friction points now in the next sprint backlog.

SOLUTION

KadhaiSolai — the product

A web-first Tamil audiobook discovery platform with three core pillars: a curated catalog with narrator quality ratings, personal library tracking, and community recommendations.

The prototype includes user auth, Tamil-language audiobook catalog, 5-star rating system with reviews, listening progress tracking, and a recommendation feed based on listening history.
